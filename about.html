Our Story

GlanceName was born from a simple observation: finding the perfect name is one of the most challenging yet important creative tasks. Whether you're naming a business, a character, a pet, or even a baby, the right name can make all the difference.

Founded in 2023, our team of naming enthusiasts, linguists, and AI specialists came together to create a tool that would simplify this process for everyone. We believe that everyone deserves access to creative name generation tools without barriers or costs.

Today, GlanceName has helped over 500,000 users find their perfect names, and we're just getting started!

Our Mission

At GlanceName, our mission is to democratize creativity by providing powerful, accessible tools that help people bring their ideas to life through the power of naming.

We believe that:

  • Creative tools should be available to everyone, regardless of budget
  • The naming process should be fun, inspiring, and efficient
  • Technology should enhance human creativity, not replace it
  • Privacy and user experience should never be compromised
